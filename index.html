FAU Capstone Project - NYPD Arrest Data

This site hosts all deliverables for the Florida Atlantic University Capstone project that focuses on mapping and predicting offenses while utilizing NYPD arrest data.

This project aims to identify and predict the probability of the most prominent offense types for each precinct within New York City for each day of the week, providing actionable insights for local law enforcement agencies and communities.
